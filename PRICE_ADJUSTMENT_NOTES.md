# Property Price Adjustments - Market Research

## Price Updates Made

Based on 2024-2025 Japanese luxury real estate market research, I've adjusted prices to reflect realistic premium property values:

### Previous vs. Updated Prices:

1. **Tokyo Minato-ku Penthouse**
   - Previous: ¥145,000,000 (~$950K USD)
   - Updated: ¥285,000,000 (~$1.86M USD)
   - **Reason**: Minato-ku (Roppongi/Azabu) is one of Tokyo's most premium areas. Luxury penthouses typically range ¥200M-¥500M+.

2. **Kyoto Traditional Estate**
   - Previous: ¥98,000,000 (~$640K USD)
   - Updated: ¥185,000,000 (~$1.21M USD)
   - **Reason**: Higashiyama-ku is Kyoto's most prestigious area. Renovated traditional machiya estates in prime locations typically range ¥150M-¥300M+.

3. **Osaka Luxury Apartment**
   - Previous: ¥78,000,000 (~$510K USD)
   - Updated: ¥125,000,000 (~$820K USD)
   - **Reason**: Chuo-ku is Osaka's premium district. Luxury apartments typically range ¥100M-¥200M.

4. **Shibuya Modern Loft**
   - Previous: ¥125,000,000 (~$820K USD)
   - Updated: ¥245,000,000 (~$1.6M USD)
   - **Reason**: Shibuya is a high-end area. Luxury modern lofts typically range ¥200M-¥400M.

5. **Hakone Mountain Villa**
   - Previous: ¥165,000,000 (~$1.08M USD)
   - Updated: ¥325,000,000 (~$2.13M USD)
   - **Reason**: Hakone is a premium resort area. Large luxury villas typically range ¥250M-¥500M+.

6. **Ginza Premium Suite**
   - Previous: ¥195,000,000 (~$1.28M USD)
   - Updated: ¥395,000,000 (~$2.58M USD)
   - **Reason**: Ginza is Tokyo's most exclusive area. Premium suites typically range ¥300M-¥1B+.

## Market Context (2024-2025)

- **Tokyo luxury market**: Prices rising ~8% annually
- **Land prices**: Up 2.7% in 2024 (strongest since 1991)
- **Foreign investment**: 27% of transactions, up to 40% in central Tokyo
- **Premium areas**: Minato-ku, Ginza, Shibuya command highest prices
- **Tourism impact**: Strong demand for luxury holiday homes

## Price Ranges by Area (Luxury Segment)

- **Ginza**: ¥300M-¥1B+ (ultra-premium)
- **Minato-ku**: ¥200M-¥500M+ (premium)
- **Shibuya**: ¥200M-¥400M (high-end)
- **Kyoto Higashiyama**: ¥150M-¥300M+ (traditional luxury)
- **Hakone**: ¥250M-¥500M+ (resort luxury)
- **Osaka Chuo-ku**: ¥100M-¥200M (premium)

All updated prices now fall within realistic ranges for luxury properties in these premium locations.
